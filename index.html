What we do

Frequently asked questions

Know what you are signing up for.

The founder video contains the full role explanation. These answers cover the basics before you begin.

Is this cold calling?

You will speak with local business owners who have received or are connected to an anticipated website preview. Some conversations begin from expressed interest; others may require you to create interest and explain why you are calling.

Do I close the sale or collect payment?

No. Your job is to build rapport, answer approved general questions, send the correct details, and book a qualified presentation. The owner handles the complete presentation and payment.

What does training look like?

Training includes a paid role-play session, live-call coaching, and hands-on use of the CRM, dialer, and time-tracking tools.

What tools will I use?

You should expect to use a CRM, a browser-based dialer, and Hubstaff. The exact setup and login process are covered during onboarding.

What are the working hours?

The role operates during U.S. Eastern Time. Enter your exact availability in the application so we can verify that it overlaps with the required calling window.

Can I work multiple jobs?

You must be fully available, responsive, and focused during your scheduled SolidBooked Pro hours. Any other commitment cannot interfere with attendance, call quality, follow-up, or performance.

Do I work weekends?

The standard schedule and any optional weekend needs will be explained in the founder video and confirmed during the interview.

How can I advance?

High-performing website setters may be considered for annuity appointment setting, annuity closing, or customer-experience roles. Advancement depends on consistent results and reliability.
