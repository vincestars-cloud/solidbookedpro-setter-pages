What we do

We help business owners get more customers and clients.

Low Ticket

We build them a website and get them ranked online. Our model is show first then sell. So we build and prepare everything first, send it to them and if they are interested we book an appointment with them for them to pay.

High Ticket

We run ad campaigns and send them qualified customers. Our guarantee to the business owners is, if the customer/lead does not show up, they do not pay for the lead.

What you will do

We have both warm and cold business owners that need to be contacted and scheduled on the calendar.

  • Warm prospect (40%): They received something from us and raised their hand that they are considering our service.
  • Cold prospect (60%): We identified they need our service, like a business with a website that is not working or a business that is not getting enough customers, but they have not opted in yet.

Who succeeds here

  • Comfortable speaking with cold and warm prospects all day.
  • Reliable, coachable and consistent with follow-up.
  • Can build rapport and is ok going off script.
  • Has previous experience in a similar role.

This is not for you if

  • You dislike phone conversations or avoid follow-up.
  • You have no rapport or sales training.

FAQ

What will I be doing day to day?

You will contact warm and cold business owners, build rapport, answer general questions, update the CRM, and book qualified appointments for the owner or closer.

Is this cold calling?

About 40% are warm prospects who already raised their hand. About 60% are cold prospects we identified as likely needing help getting more customers.

What kind of prospects will I call?

You will speak with business owners who have received a message from us. Some conversations begin from expressed interest; others may require you to create interest and explain why you are calling.

What does training look like?

Training includes a paid role-play session, live-call coaching, and hands-on use of the CRM, dialer, and time-tracking tools.

What tools will I use?

You will be using our CRM and dialer. We use Hubstaff for time tracking and pay. Hubstaff supports paying you via Wise, PayPal, Payoneer and several other payment providers.

How will I be paid?

We use Hubstaff for time tracking and pay. Hubstaff supports paying you via Wise, PayPal, Payoneer and several other payment providers you can choose from.

Do I work weekends?

Normal work days are Monday to Friday. Weekends are optional if you choose to work those days as well.

What are the working hours?

The role operates during U.S. Eastern Time. Enter your exact availability in the application so we can verify that it overlaps with the required calling window.

Do I close the sale or collect payment?

No. Your job is to build rapport, answer approved general questions, send the correct details, and book a qualified presentation. The owner handles the complete presentation and payment.

What should I have ready before starting?

Use a quiet device with a working microphone and have your resume, experience, availability, desired hourly pay, Vocaroo link, and past metrics ready.
